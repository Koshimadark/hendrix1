Course Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) is a four-year program that equips students with strong foundations in computing, programming, data structures, software engineering, and emerging technologies. This program is designed to prepare graduates for careers in software development, IT industry, research, and academia.

Why Choose BSCS?

Choosing BSCS means entering a dynamic and ever-growing field where technology drives innovation. This program provides not just theoretical knowledge but also practical experience in solving real-world problems. It opens opportunities in industries like software development, artificial intelligence, data science, and cybersecurity.

Offered Programs

  • BSCS Major in Artificial Intelligence
  • BSCS Major in Data Science
  • BSCS Major in Software Engineering
  • BSCS Major in Cybersecurity
  • BSCS General Track

Why It's Fun to Enroll

Enrolling in BSCS is not just about coding—it’s about collaboration, creativity, and innovation. Students enjoy hands-on projects, coding competitions, hackathons, research presentations, and group activities that make learning exciting and rewarding. The program builds strong friendships, teamwork, and real-world skills while having fun along the way.

Curriculum Highlights

Programming

Learn programming languages such as Python, Java, and C++ to build real-world applications.

Data Structures & Algorithms

Develop problem-solving skills with efficient algorithms and data organization techniques.

Software Engineering

Understand system design, development methodologies, and project management.

Artificial Intelligence

Explore machine learning, robotics, and intelligent systems shaping the future of technology.
